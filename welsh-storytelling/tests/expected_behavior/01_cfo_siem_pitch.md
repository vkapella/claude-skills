# Expected behavior — 01 CFO SIEM pitch

## Voice
- **Inferred (not asked):** Boardroom.
- The interrogation message should state the inference: something like "Reading this as Boardroom voice — confident, decision-forcing, dollars in the headline."

## Structure
- 5 slides + appendix (matches the user's explicit "5-slide" request and the 30-minute slot).
- 5-Beat arc (Setup → What → How → Why → Magic Wand) — default.

## Headline character
- The What-slide sentence should contain a number and a date, e.g. "Move to Microsoft Sentinel by end of Q3 — saves $600K over three years and fixes the two SOC blind spots from our last audit."
- The Magic Wand should end with a three-beat close like "Decision this week. Pilot in 30 days. Cutover by Q3."
- "Either option works" or any hedge in the close = fail.

- `.pptx` deck and `.docx` Story Guide. Both presented via `present_files`.
