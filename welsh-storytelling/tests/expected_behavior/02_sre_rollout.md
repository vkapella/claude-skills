# Expected behavior — 02 SRE rollout

## Voice
- **Inferred (not asked):** Operator.
- The interrogation message should state the inference: something like "Reading this as Operator voice — plainspoken, leads with what changes Monday morning."

## Structure
- 3-5 slides (≤15-min slot bracket bumped to 20 min — same range).
- 5-Beat arc default; Setup focused on "what's changing for you," not on stakes or cost.

## Headline character
- Headlines are direct and conversational. NOT three-beat. NOT decision-forcing.
- The What-slide should be a sentence like "Opsgenie replaces PagerDuty starting [date] — same on-call schedule, slightly different acknowledge flow, two things you'll notice in week one."
- The Magic Wand should be operational, not strategic: "Pilot starts Monday. Office hours Thursday. Ping me on Slack if anything feels weird."

## Failure modes to watch for
- Boardroom three-beat close ("Sign this week. Ship by July. ...") in a peer rollout = fail.
- Strategic-sounding language ("force multiplier," "paradigm shift") = fail.
- Pretending the rollout is smoother than it is = fail. Honest "this part is still rough" callouts are correct.

## Both files produced
- `.pptx` and `.docx`. The Story Guide should also be in Operator register — verbatim opening should be conversational, not a podium-ready promise.
